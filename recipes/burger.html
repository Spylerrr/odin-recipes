Burger

The best burgers are made by you

Summertime is for grilling and this bruger recipe is the best for it! It will tackle that hunger!

Ingredients

  • 1½ pounds lean ground beef
  • ½ onion, finley chopped
  • ½ cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup
  • 1 glove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp ried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • toppings of choice
    •

    How to make it

    1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate
    2. Meanwhile, combine ground beef, onion, chees, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsely, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
    3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
    4. Place patty into a bun with toppings of choice
